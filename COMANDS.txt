Επισης πρεπει να βαλεις το path του json file στο functions.py line,79)




ENGLISH




Orders:

After you open the ignite.py you have to report the word <open> and then if you report the command <wait mode> it goes into standby mode and you have to say <open> again to open

SIMPLE CONTENT

HEY, HEY, WHERE ARE YOU?

WHAT ARE YOU DOING, HOW ARE YOU

WHO MADE YOU

WHAT'S YOUR NAME?

you can ask him to tell you joke

you can ask him to tell you the time and date


APPLICATION OPENING


can you open youtube as long as you mention the word
youtube

can you open google as long as you mention the word google or browser

can open music player program as long as you mention the word music or song or music player (in file main.py line, 40 you have to put the path of one of your songs P.O D:/Music/System of a Down - Chop Suey!.mp3)

you can enter a note as long as you mention the word note or notebook and after you are asked to dictate the text you want(the TXT file is stored in the same folder as the codes)


OPENING OF SERVICES


by referring to the word weather or temperature you activate the information about the weather for the city of Patras: 1) today or now-> for today, 2 )next or next - > for future(you can change the parameters in functions.py line 22 ,23 for different location)

at the mention of the word search or search searches on the web

with the mention of the word calendar it connects to google calendar and shows you the facts you have for the day you ask(because it works with api you need to create your own api key from google calendar and put the json file with the credits in the same folder as the codes. You also need to put the path of json file in functions.py line, 79)

ATTENTION: in order to use it with english language you have to replace all the greek words with their translation in english. You also have to replace the language parameter in helping.py line 7, 21(see https://pypi.org/project/SpeechRecognition/)
